----------------------------------------
   Open Source Manifesto
----------------------------------------
Date: 30 March 2026
Author: lenovo

I believe in the power of open source and collective innovation.
Every day, I rely on tools like git, which represent collaboration and shared knowledge.
To me, freedom means choice — the ability to learn, modify, and improve without restriction.
In the future, I aspire to build a student toolkit and share it freely with the world,
so that others can learn, adapt, and grow from it just as I have.

I stand for openness, transparency, and community-driven development.
